		### Source Notes and Caveats 
		- GDP per head at current market prices time series includes the effects of inflation and is most useful for comparison of levels across different areas.
		
		- GDP per head at current market prices time series have the effects of inflation removed by considering changes in quantity between consecutive periods, holding prices from previous periods constant and is most useful for monitoring growth over time within any given area.
		
		- 2022 data are provisional.
